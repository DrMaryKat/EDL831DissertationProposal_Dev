Overview

Research questions bridge your problem statement and methodology. This week you'll transform your problem into specific, investigable questions using established frameworks and assess their feasibility.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1
Transform problems into specific research questions
2
Apply PICO and SPICE frameworks
3
Distinguish between question types and methodological implications
4
Assess question feasibility systematically
5
Develop aligned primary and sub-questions

Independent Work Tasks

Complete these tasks and check them off as you go:

Draft Initial Questions

Write 3-5 potential research questions based on your problem statement.

Apply Framework Analysis

Use PICO (quantitative) or SPICE (qualitative) to structure questions.

Conduct Feasibility Assessment

Evaluate using ACCESS framework (Access, Cost, Complexity, Ethics, Scope, Significance).

Finalize Question Set

Select and polish primary question and 2-4 sub-questions.
