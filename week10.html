Phase 3: Chapter 3 Development

Overview

This week focuses on the specific instruments and procedures you'll use to collect data. Your instruments must align with your research questions and design.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1
Design aligned data collection instruments
2
Develop protocols, surveys, or guides
3
Plan data collection procedures
4
Address instrument validity/reliability
5
Create a data collection timeline

Independent Work Tasks

Complete these tasks and check them off as you go:

Develop Instruments

Create your primary data collection instrument(s).

Pilot Test

If possible, pilot instruments with a small group.

Write Procedures

Document step-by-step data collection procedures.

Create Timeline

Develop a realistic data collection timeline.
