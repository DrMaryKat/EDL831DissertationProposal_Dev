Overview

Your sampling strategy determines who you'll study and affects your findings' generalizability or transferability. This week you'll develop a comprehensive sampling plan.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1
Select appropriate sampling strategies
2
Determine and justify sample size
3
Develop inclusion/exclusion criteria
4
Plan participant recruitment
5
Address access and gatekeepers

Independent Work Tasks

Complete these tasks and check them off as you go:

Select Sampling Strategy

Choose appropriate probability or non-probability sampling methods.

Determine Sample Size

Calculate or justify appropriate sample size.

Develop Criteria

Write clear inclusion and exclusion criteria.

Plan Recruitment

Outline specific strategies for identifying and recruiting participants.
