Overview

The hallmark of doctoral-level writing is synthesis‚Äîthe ability to identify patterns, draw connections, and build arguments across multiple sources. This week you'll develop these critical skills and begin structuring your Chapter 2.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1
Distinguish between summary, analysis, and synthesis
2
Identify themes and patterns across multiple sources
3
Develop synthesis paragraphs that advance arguments
4
Create an organizational structure for Chapter 2
5
Apply scholarly voice to literature discussion

Independent Work Tasks

Complete these tasks and check them off as you go:

Complete Synthesis Matrix

Populate your matrix with 25+ sources, identifying themes, methodologies, findings, and gaps.

Practice Synthesis Writing

Write 3-4 synthesis paragraphs integrating multiple sources around common themes.

Draft Chapter 2 Outline

Create a detailed outline showing thematic organization of your literature review.

Identify Critical Stance

Determine your scholarly position relative to the existing literature.
