Overview

This week marks the beginning of your proposal development journey. Building on the foundation you established in EDL 830 last semester, you'll review your previous work, identify areas needing strengthening, and create a personalized timeline for completing your dissertation proposal.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1
Understand the structure and expectations of EDL 831
2
Review and assess your EDL 830 deliverables for proposal readiness
3
Identify gaps in your foundation work requiring remediation
4
Establish your dissertation timeline and milestone targets
5
Schedule regular check-in meetings with your professor

Independent Work Tasks

Complete these tasks and check them off as you go:

Review EDL 830 Portfolio

Gather all materials from EDL 830: research interest statement, preliminary literature review, problem identification work, and any methodology explorations.

Conduct Self-Assessment

Honestly evaluate which areas are strong and which need additional development before you can proceed with proposal writing.

Create Semester Timeline

Draft a week-by-week timeline from now through your target defense date, including all major milestones.

Schedule Professor Meeting

Arrange your first one-on-one meeting to discuss your timeline, goals, and any concerns.
