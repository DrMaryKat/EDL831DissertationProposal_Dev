Phase 1: Foundations & Chapter 2

Overview

Your theoretical and conceptual frameworks provide the intellectual scaffolding for your entire study. This week you'll solidify your framework selection, develop a visual representation, and draft this critical section of Chapter 2.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

1
Distinguish between theoretical and conceptual frameworks
2
Select and justify an appropriate theoretical framework
3
Develop a conceptual framework diagram
4
Align framework with research questions and methodology
5
Draft the framework section of Chapter 2

Independent Work Tasks

Complete these tasks and check them off as you go:

Research Framework Options

Explore 2-3 potential theoretical frameworks and evaluate their fit with your research problem.

Justify Framework Selection

Write a detailed rationale for your chosen framework.

Create Visual Diagram

Develop a conceptual framework diagram showing variables and relationships.

Draft Framework Section

Write the theoretical/conceptual framework section (5-8 pages).
